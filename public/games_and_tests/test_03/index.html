Welcome!

PREMANTURA (10 km from Pula) is a picturesque town located in Istria's far south, which was already settled during the Bronze Age. The remains of rustic villas are evidence of the ancient Romans' wise selection of Premantura as an ideal living space. The long, narrow peninsula of Kamenjak gives Premantura it's most recognizable characteristic. This peninsula is 9,5 km long and as wide as 1,5 km and stretches into the Adriatic Sea like a long arm. Kamenjak encircles a 30 km coastline dotted with numerous inlets and miniature beaches, and this primeval beauty is augmented by small island which arise from the crystalline blue-green sea.
